# Cuisine directions reflect biases in recipe corpus

**Source:** conversation

Epicure's 8 cuisine directions are learned from a multilingual but not balanced corpus. If certain cuisines are overrepresented (e.g., Western recipes dominating), the directions may conflate distinct food cultures or underrepresent others. A user from an underrepresented cuisine gets a profile that doesn't reflect their actual palate, feeling excluded or mischaracterized.
